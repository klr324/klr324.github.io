GeoJourney

GeoJourney was an introductory field program offered at Bowling Green State University. We were in the field for nine-weeks in the summer and traveled across the United States making stops at many national parks and monuments.

The program was worth 18 college credits with the courses ranging from Geology and environmental science to American culture studies:

  • GEOL 2500: Field-based Physical Geology
  • GEOL 2510: Field-based Historical Geology
  • ACS 2520: Indigenous Cultures of North America
  • ENVS 2530: Environments in Context
  • HNRS 3000: Intorduction to Critical Thinking

I attended GeoJourney several years ago as part of the 2011 cohort. It was certainly the experience of a lifetime. GeoJourney was not just a trip or a set of classes, but truly an experience. I encourage others to take part in experiences like this. Unfortunately, this program is no longer being offered at Bowling Green.
